Greenminds Environmental Sustainability Foundation (RC: 7252526) is a youth-driven NGO dedicated to building a cleaner and healthier environment. Founded in 2019 at Yaba College of Technology by passionate Environmental Biology students, we began as a campus movement tackling pollution and raising awareness. Today, Greenminds is a registered foundation empowering communities through environmental education, recycling and upcycling initiatives, capacity-building workshops, and community projects. We engage schools, neighborhoods, and young people to inspire lasting environmental responsibility. Our mission is to empower minds and sustain the future by driving action against pollution, promoting sustainable practices, and opening pathways for green careers. Through collaboration with schools, NGOs, and organizations, we co-create impactful solutions that fuel both local and global change. Vision: A cleaner, healthier, and sustainable environment powered by youth, innovation, and collective action.
