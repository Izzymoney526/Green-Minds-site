Who We Are

Founded in 2019 at Yabatech, Green Minds Environmental Sustainability Foundation (RC: 7252526) is a youth-driven organization committed to environmental sustainability, climate awareness, and eco-innovation. We empower young people to create sustainable solutions for Nigeria's environmental challenges through education, mentorship, and community engagement.
